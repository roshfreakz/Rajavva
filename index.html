What Are We

Teach! Learn! Act! - Lately, these have just become desolate words which are supposedly three very dependent ones. The Rajavva Academy desires to knit them together not just as mere words rather a bond between the teachers, students and the society enveloping them.

At R-Acad we believe in guiding our students to think rationally, rise up both intellectually and socially conscious and most importantly achieve goals in their own fashion
